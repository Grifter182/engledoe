Funeral Planning

Planning a funeral in the Western Cape involves more than choosing a date. Our compassionate team handles every aspect—from venue bookings to official documentation—so you can focus on honouring your loved one’s life.

How We Help

  • Venue selection and bookings across Cape Town’s churches, halls, gardens and scenic locations
  • Catering and refreshments coordination with trusted local suppliers
  • Transportation for family, guests and the deceased
  • Floral arrangements, décor and audio‑visual services
  • Design and printing of programmes and memorial stationery

Why Plan Ahead?

Having a plan in place relieves your family of financial and logistical stress. By documenting your wishes, you ensure the service reflects your beliefs and culture. We offer pre‑planning consultations at no cost.

Documents & Logistics

We assist with registering the death, obtaining the BI‑1663 certificate and arranging burial or cremation permits. Our team coordinates with municipalities across the Western Cape to secure venues and ensure all cultural and religious protocols are respected.

Guidance & Support

From the first call through to the day of the service, we provide step‑by‑step guidance. Our planners are familiar with customs across Cape Town’s diverse communities and will tailor every detail accordingly.

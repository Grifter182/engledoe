Burial Services

In Cape Town and across the Western Cape we arrange traditional and personalised burial ceremonies that respect your family’s customs. We’ll help secure a burial plot despite limited cemetery space, handle every detail and honour the life of your loved one.

What’s Included

  • Advice on cemetery selection across Cape Town and the Western Cape
  • Collection and respectful care of your loved one
  • Planning of graveside services and memorial receptions to reflect your culture
  • Assistance with documentation, including the BI‑1663 medical certificate and burial permits
  • Floral arrangements, printed materials and obituary notices

Documents Required

  • Medical certificate of cause of death (Form BI‑1663)
  • Identity document of the deceased
  • Residential address and personal details

Eco‑Friendly Burial Options

We can arrange environmentally friendly burials using wooden coffins and avoiding metal or chemical glues to minimise environmental impact.

Pricing Guidance

Costs vary based on grave fees, service location and catering. We’ll provide a transparent quote tailored to your needs and can help you lock in today’s prices through pre‑planning.

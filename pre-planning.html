Pre‑Planning & Funeral Cover

Planning ahead in Cape Town and across the Western Cape ensures your wishes are respected and spares your family from financial stress. Our family‑run team will walk you through funeral cover options, cremation or burial decisions and eco‑friendly choices that honour local traditions.

Benefits of Pre‑Planning

  • Lock in today’s prices for future services
  • Ensure your preferences are documented
  • Provide peace of mind for your family
  • Simplify decision‑making during emotional times

Why Pre‑Plan in the Western Cape?

Cape Town’s cemeteries have limited space, and costs continue to rise. Pre‑planning allows you to secure a burial plot or choose cremation while locking in today’s prices and reducing stress on your loved ones.

Documents Required

  • Medical certificate of cause of death (Form BI‑1663)
  • Identity document of the deceased
  • Residential address and personal details
  • Any applicable funeral policy documents

Eco‑Friendly Options

We support environmentally sensitive funerals by offering biodegradable or wooden coffins and avoiding metal or chemical glues, as recommended by the City of Cape Town.

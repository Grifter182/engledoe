What We Offer

Comprehensive services designed to honour your loved ones with dignity, respect, and compassionate care.

Burial Services

Traditional and personalised burial arrangements conducted with the utmost dignity.

Our team assists with selecting a cemetery, arranging transportation, organising a graveside service and handling legal documentation.

Cremation Services

Respectful cremation services with options for memorials and ash scattering.

We offer affordable and dignified cremations, with choices for direct cremation or a service, allowing ashes to be kept in an urn, scattered or placed in a columbarium.

Funeral Planning

Thoughtful planning and coordination to ensure every detail is taken care of.

We manage every detail—venue booking, catering, transportation, floral arrangements and printed materials—ensuring a seamless, stress‑free experience.

Full Parlour Services

Complete arrangements, logistics and compassionate on‑the‑day care.

Our parlour team offers comprehensive coordination, compassionate support and customised arrangements, drawing on our professional expertise and personalised approach.

A Legacy of Care Since 1955

Rooted in Cape Town for generations, WS Engledoe & Sons is a family‑run funeral home built on compassion, integrity and respect. Since 1955 we've been trusted to walk alongside families with quiet professionalism—caring for every detail so each farewell genuinely reflects the life it honours.

From our base in Salt River, our experienced team offers calm, personalised guidance—whether you need immediate assistance or thoughtful planning for the days ahead. We uphold values of dignity, respect and compassion and provide tailored services to honour every life.
